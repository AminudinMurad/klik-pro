# Klik PRO 1.4.4

Klik PRO 1.4.4 redesigns the Mappings tab so your mouse and its button
assignments read as a single, self-contained profile, and brings the Horizontal
Thumb Wheel's browser control directly into the tab.

- **Mouse Profile card.** The mouse and all four of its button callouts now live
  together in one bordered **Mouse Profile** panel, clearly separated from the app
  lists below, with each callout sitting beside the button it controls.
- **Compact, stacked button controls.** Every mouse button's action picker and its
  shortcut or app picker are now stacked and sized to their content, tucked in
  around the mouse so the whole profile is easy to take in at a glance.
- **Horizontal Thumb Wheel in Mappings.** The thumb wheel now appears as its own
  control in the Mappings tab, with a browser selector (All browsers, or a specific
  set). Your choice stays in sync with the browser checkboxes in Settings — change
  it in either place and both update.
- **Refresh App List.** The control that rescans your installed apps and profiles
  now carries a clear refresh icon and sits directly above the Native Apps and App
  Profiles lists.

Your existing button assignments and shortcuts are unchanged — this release
reorganizes how the Mappings tab presents them.
